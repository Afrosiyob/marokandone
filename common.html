Common education


Three terms a year are nowhere else; your time is precious, don't waste! TOBB ETU student completes his undergraduate education in 4 years, with approximately 1 year of work experience. We call this experience gained in 3 workplaces until graduation, as Cooperative Education. Since our system is designed specifically for Cooperative Education, we provide training for 3 terms a year. Moreover, since the last year has been studying for 2 terms, TOBB ETÜ students graduate earlier than other university students.
